Refund Policy:
All purchases are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date. After 30 days, a partial refund of 50% may be issued at the company's discretion. Digital products are eligible for refund only if they have not been downloaded or accessed. To request a refund, customers must contact support with their order number and reason for the refund. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after approval.

---

Shipping Policy:
Standard shipping takes 5 to 7 business days within the continental United States. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and delivers within 2 to 3 business days. International shipping takes 10 to 15 business days depending on the destination country. Free shipping is available on all orders over 50 dollars. Tracking information is sent via email once the order has been shipped.

---

Account Management:
To reset your password, click the Forgot Password link on the login page and follow the email instructions. Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and include one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. Accounts that have been inactive for 12 months will be automatically deactivated. To reactivate a deactivated account, contact customer support with your registered email address. Two-factor authentication is available and can be enabled from the Security Settings page.

---

Subscription Plans:
The Basic plan costs 9.99 dollars per month and includes access to core features. The Pro plan costs 29.99 dollars per month and includes all Basic features plus priority support and advanced analytics. The Enterprise plan is custom-priced and includes dedicated account management, custom integrations, and SLA guarantees. All plans include a 14-day free trial. Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time, with changes taking effect at the next billing cycle.

---

Technical Support:
Technical support is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time. Premium support customers have access to 24/7 support via phone and chat. Response times for standard support tickets are within 24 hours. Critical issues affecting service availability are prioritized and addressed within 4 hours. A knowledge base with troubleshooting guides is available at support.example.com.
